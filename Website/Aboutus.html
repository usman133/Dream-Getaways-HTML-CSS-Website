*** Who we are ***

Dream Getaways Resort is a self-contained commercial establishment that endeavors to provide most of a vacationer's wants such as food, lodging, sports, entertainment etc, with the best quality. Dream Getaways Resort is the perfect destination, where you can feel the true nature of hospitality. Our goal is to provide the best experience possible so you can always come back. We have always focused on providing high-quality food, accommodation, transportation and lots of it, at a very reasonable price with fast and excellent service, in outstanding settings.


*** What we deliver ***

Everything we do at Dream Getaways Resort, we believe in providing visitors with a one-of-a-kind geothermal experience. Dream Getaways Resort is the largest international 4-star Resort in Wellington, New Zealand and the resort still enjoys a wonderful historic and relaxing atmosphere. We believe in providing visitors with modern hotel room and villa accommodation, abundant indoor and outdoor leisure facilities and exquisite New Zealand focused dining options.

Dream Getaways Resort is very popular among locals as well as visitors from around New Zealand and around the world. Advance bookings are strongly recommended.
